Achievements

Rose has earned numerous accolades as a member of BLACKPINK and as a solo artist. Here are some of her career highlights:

2021 Billboard Music Award

Rose received the Top Social Artist award as part of BLACKPINK. This achievement is a testament to the group's worldwide influence and fanbase.

Solo Debut: "On The Ground"

Her solo single "On The Ground" was a major success, reaching No. 1 on the Billboard Digital Song Sales chart. This achievement marked a milestone in Rose's solo career.

MTV Europe Music Awards - Best Korean Act

Rose has been recognized as the Best Korean Act multiple times, further solidifying her influence in the global music scene.
